Objective

An opportunity to improve oneself, as well as the lives of the people that I cross. I'm presently a Drug Product Planning Specialist at a large, biopharmaceutical company. My main responsibility is to ensure that my products meet market requirements, so customers can receive the medication required to address their life threatening conditions.
